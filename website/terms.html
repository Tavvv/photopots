These Terms govern your use of PhotoPots, an app published by OhhWowLabs.

The app and your data

PhotoPots organizes photos on your Apple devices. The app reads photos you explicitly select, copies them into a per-app sandbox, and (with your confirmation) removes them from your system Photos library. Data syncs across your devices through your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. We do not host a server, and we have no access to your photos, pots, tags, or search history.

You are responsible for maintaining backups of your photos. PhotoPots' local sandbox is included in your iCloud backup if you have iCloud Backup enabled, and the metadata syncs through your private CloudKit database — but we strongly recommend keeping independent backups of irreplaceable photos.

Subscriptions and purchases

PhotoPots is free to install and includes a weekly limit on photo moves. PhotoPots Pro is an auto-renewing subscription that removes the limit:

  • Monthly: $2.99/month with a 7-day free trial for new subscribers
  • Annual: $29.99/year

Subscriptions auto-renew at the end of each billing period unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the period ends. You can manage and cancel subscriptions in Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on iPhone, or System Settings → [your name] → Media & Purchases → Manage on Mac. The free trial converts to a paid subscription automatically unless cancelled before it ends.

Offboarding Pass is a one-time non-consumable purchase of $1.99 that unlocks unlimited moves for 30 days from purchase. It does not auto-renew.

All purchases are processed by Apple and are subject to the Apple Media Services Terms. Refund requests go to Apple, not to us — see reportaproblem.apple.com.

Acceptable use

You may use PhotoPots to organize photos that you have the right to possess and process on your own devices. Don't use the app to handle content that's illegal in your jurisdiction.
