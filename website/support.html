    Read the getting started answers in the FAQ →

  • Restore purchases

    Bought Pro and it's not showing on a new phone or your Mac? It's tied to your Apple ID, so it's quick to bring back:

    1. Open PhotoPots and go to Settings.
    2. Tap Restore purchases.
    3. Sign in with the Apple ID you used to buy Pro.

    Still stuck? Email us and we'll sort it out.

  • iCloud sync troubles

    Pots sync across iPhone, iPad, and Mac through iCloud. If something's not showing up everywhere, work through these in order:

    1. Check you're signed into the same Apple ID on every device (Settings → your name).
    2. Make sure iCloud Drive is on for PhotoPots (Settings → your name → iCloud).
    3. Connect to Wi-Fi and leave the app open for a minute — big pots sync in the background.
    4. Restart the app. Still nothing after an hour? Tell us which device is missing what.
